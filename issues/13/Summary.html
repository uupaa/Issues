Summary

iOS 9 Safari WebAudio::AudioBufferSourceNode does not play in touchstart event.

OS Version

iOS 9.0 - 9.1 beta

Devices

iPhone 4s and iPhone 5. I have not tried in the other device.

What steps will reproduce the problem?

  1. Open https://uupaa.github.io/Issues/issues/13/index.html in your iOS Device.
  2. Screen should become green or red. Green uses touchend, red uses a touchstart.
  3. White noise is played when you touch the text. If the screen is red, does not play it.

What is the expected behavior?

AFAIK current playback regulation is User Control of Downloads Over Cellular Networks.

I hope to more clearly the iOS Safari media playback specifications.

What went wrong?

There is a difference in behavior in iOS 8 and iOS 9.
